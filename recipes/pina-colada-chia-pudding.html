Pina Colada Chia Pudding

Breakfast

Ingredients

  • 250g Ayam Light Coconut Milk
  • 100g Golden Circle Crushed Pineapple in Juice (drained)
  • 20g Macro Black Chia Seeds
  • 30g NU Harvest Vanilla Pancake Batter Plant Protein
  • 5g Queen Sugar Free Maple Flavoured Syrup
  • 10g Woolworths Shredded Coconut

Steps

  1. In a bowl or jar, whisk together the coconut milk, protein powder, and maple syrup until smooth.
  2. Stir in the crushed pineapple and chia seeds.
  3. Divide the mixture evenly between two jars or containers.
  4. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight, until thickened.
  5. Top with shredded coconut just before serving.
