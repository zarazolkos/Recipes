Smashed Avo On Toast

Breakfast or Snack

Ingredients

  • 77g Herman Brot Complete Protein Bread (Approx. 2 Slices)
  • 70g ripe avocado
  • 5mL lemon juice
  • 1g salt
  • 1g pepper
  • 2g chilli flakes
  • 2g hemp seeds

Steps

  1. Toast the bread to your preferred level.
  2. In a bowl, mash the avocado with lemon juice, salt, and pepper until combined.
  3. Spread the avocado mixture evenly over the toast.
  4. Sprinkle with chilli flakes and hemp seeds.
  5. Serve immediately.
