Korean Turkey Bowl

Easy Eats

Ingredients

  • 110g Ingham's Turkey Breast Mince
  • 5mL Ayam Pure Sesame Oil
  • 40mL Ottogi Korean BBQ Bulgogi Sauce
  • 125g SunRice Microwave Jasmine Rice Cup
  • 30g cucumber, peeled into ribbons
  • 30g carrot, shredded or grated
  • 10g spring onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 large egg (50g)
  • 3g sesame seeds
  • 5mL Flying Goose Sriracha Sauce Original

Steps

  1. Heat sesame oil in a non-stick frypan over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the turkey mince, breaking it up with a spatula. Cook for 3-4 minutes until browned.
  3. Pour in the Korean BBQ sauce and stir through. Cook for a further 2-3 minutes until glossy and well coated.
  4. Meanwhile, microwave the rice according to packet instructions.
  5. In a sperate non-stick frypan, fry the egg to your liking.
  6. Use a vegetable peeler to create cucumber ribbons, shred or grate the carrot, and thinly slice the spring onion.
  7. Add the fried egg on top. Sprinle with sesame seeds and drizzle with sriracha before serving.
